Putting in relation

Ready for a simple visualization game? Approximately 2500 trees grow per hectare in the tropical rainforest. However, on average, only a single mahogany tree grows in the same area. When cutting and salvaging one mahogany tree (red dot), you have to search and transport between 2499 other trees (black dots).
