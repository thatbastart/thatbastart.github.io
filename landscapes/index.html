Savior?

Mahogany plantations offer the advantage of not having to cut destructive paths into the landscape because the trees were planted knowing they could be harvested later. These plantations exceed the natural density of mahogany trees in a forest, but the teeming ecosystems that thrive beneath their tall canopies are preserved.
