Deforestation and Cultivation Companies

Mahogany's natural distribution areas are not only home to natural stands: Deforestation and Cultivation Companies have recognized the advantage of perfect weather conditions and offer customers the opportunity to grow timber directly in South America.
These companies sell mahogany cultivation as an investment and not infrequently use a narrative like “We protect and grow your money”.

TREE PLANTATION

This is a company that has adopted this very slogan. They promise the fastest way to build wealth, combat climate change, create jobs and rebuild damaged ecosystems by planting instant forests.

They also advertise with stunning pictures of plantings and, of course, outstanding new ways of growing. TREE PLANTATION swears by Crop Circle cultivation.
