The decreasing demand of tropical wood and mahogany in particular did not lead to an end of the related colonial style in industrialised countries. Wood stain in the colour “mahogany” allows the emulation of mahogany-wood through coloring any other light-colored wood. A derivation of the wood stain “mahogany color” is a wood stain named “colonial color”: It aims to emphasize its suitability to achieve the colonial style-look for the colored item and is completely detached from the referred matter, the wood species.
